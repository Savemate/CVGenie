Cookie Policy

9to5 University | Last Updated: January 2024

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our career education platform. They help make 9to5 University work more efficiently and provide information to help us improve your learning experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

9to5 University uses cookies for educational and platform functionality purposes:

2.1 Essential Educational Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the platform to function properly for your career education:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
learning_session Maintains your learning session and course progress Session
career_profile Saves your resume and career profile data temporarily Session
consent_status Remembers your cookie consent preferences 1 year

2.2 Functional Learning Cookies

These cookies enhance your learning experience and platform functionality:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
course_progress Tracks your progress through career courses 30 days
template_preference Remembers your preferred resume template style 30 days
language_preference Stores your language preference for the platform 1 year

2.3 Analytics & Improvement Cookies

These cookies help us understand how learners use our platform:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics - Distinguishes users for learning analytics 2 years
_gid Google Analytics - Distinguishes users for daily tracking 24 hours
_gat Google Analytics - Used to throttle request rate 1 minute

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may also use various third-party cookies to enhance your learning experience:

  • Google Analytics: For understanding how learners use our career education platform
  • Font Awesome: For displaying educational icons and interface elements
  • Google Fonts: For displaying readable text in our courses
  • jsPDF/html2canvas: For resume download functionality

4. Your Cookie Choices (POPIA Compliance)

Under South African data protection laws (POPIA), you have control over cookies:

4.1 Browser Controls

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings:

  • Delete all cookies stored on your device
  • Block all or some cookies
  • Clear cookies when you close your browser
  • Receive notifications when cookies are set

However, please note that if you block all cookies, some features of 9to5 University may not work properly for your career education.

4.2 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our platform, you will see a cookie consent banner that allows you to:

  • Accept all cookies for full platform functionality
  • Reject all non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences based on your needs

You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our platform footer or by clearing your browser cookies.

5. Do Not Track Signals

9to5 University does not currently respond to Do Not Track signals from web browsers. However, you can adjust your cookie preferences using the methods described above to control tracking for your learning activities.

6. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, regulation, or our educational data practices. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies for your career education.

Significant changes will be communicated to registered users via email.

If you have any questions about our use of cookies or your cookie preferences, please contact us:
