Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

CVGenie uses cookies for several purposes:

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
session_id Maintains your session while using the resume builder Session
consent_status Remembers your cookie consent preferences 1 year

2.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
resume_data Stores your resume data temporarily in your browser Session
template_preference Remembers your preferred resume template 30 days

2.3 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics - Distinguishes users 2 years
_gid Google Analytics - Distinguishes users 24 hours

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may also use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics of the Service, deliver advertisements, and so on. These include:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and performance monitoring
  • CookieYes: For cookie consent management
  • Font Awesome: For displaying icons on our website
  • Google Fonts: For displaying custom fonts

4. Your Cookie Choices

You have several options to control or limit how we and our partners use cookies:

4.1 Browser Controls

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Delete all cookies stored on your device
  • Block all or some cookies
  • Clear cookies when you close your browser

However, please note that if you block all cookies, some features of our website may not work properly.

4.2 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner that allows you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject all non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences

You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our website footer.

5. Do Not Track Signals

Our website does not currently respond to Do Not Track signals from web browsers. However, you can adjust your cookie preferences using the methods described above.

6. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, regulation, or our data practices. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
