About 9to5 University

9to5 University bridges the gap between education and employment. We provide completely free, practical career education focused on 9-to-5 workplace challenges.

Our platform offers resume building, interview preparation, workplace conflict resolution courses, workers rights information, and access to piece job opportunities through The Hustle Block.
