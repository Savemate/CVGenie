About 9to5 University

9to5 University was created to bridge the gap between education and employment. We believe every South African deserves access to quality career education and professional development tools.

Our mission is to provide completely free, practical career education that prepares you for the 9-to-5 workplace - from resume building to interview skills, professional development to career advancement.

We focus on practical, job-ready skills that employers actually want, with content specifically tailored for the South African job market.
