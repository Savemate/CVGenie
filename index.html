About CVGenie

CVGenie was built by MagTec Invest to assist job seekers on their journey, knowing that professional resume services can cost an arm and leg for someone without income.

We believe every South African deserves access to professional tools that can help them secure employment, regardless of their background, education level, or work experience.

Our mission is simple: to provide completely free, high-quality resume building tools that work for everyone - from recent graduates to experienced professionals, from corporate roles to skilled trades.
